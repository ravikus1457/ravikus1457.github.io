Symbio Radar

Symbio Radar is a personal, single-user automation tool that creates original gaming-commentary short videos and uploads them to its owner's own YouTube channel, @TheSymbioRadar, using the YouTube Data API.

  • Single user: the channel owner (the developer).
  • Uploads only the owner's original content to the owner's own channel.
  • Reads analytics only for the owner's own channel.
  • Not distributed, sold, or offered to any third party; no third-party or viewer data is accessed or stored.
